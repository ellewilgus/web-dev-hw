Why did I choose this Project?

I chose to make an Instructable for making an Americano because it includes simple steps that anyone can follow. Since I have learned how to make an Americano at home, it has saved me time and money. You can make your favorite drink and customize it to your every desire.

Where to Learn More about the Americano

Ideas for Dressing Up Your Americano at Home

Drink How To Make It
Iced Americano Substitute cold water and add ice to a glass.
Créme Caramel Americano Add half & half and 1-2 tablespoons caramel syrup
Skinny Vanilla Americano Add fat free milk and sugar free vanilla flavoring
Shot in the Dark Replace hot water with hot coffee
