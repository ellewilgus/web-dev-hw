How To Make An Americano

What You Will Need

  • Mug
  • Espresso
  • Water
  • Optional: Flavored syrup or sweetener
  • Optional: Cream or milk of choice

Let's Get Started!

Step One


Brew the desired number of espresso shots into your mug.

Step Two


Heat water to desired temperature using a microwave or in a kettle on the stove.

Step Three


Add hot water to espresso shots, nearly filling your mug.

Step Four


Optional: Add a splash of cream or milk to your mug.

Step Five


Optional: Add sweetener or flavoring of your choice and stir.

Step Six


Enjoy your americano hot or iced by substituting cold water and adding ice!
