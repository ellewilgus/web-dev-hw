Classic Pico de Gallo

Ingredients

  • 4 cups diced red tomatoe
  • 1 cup chopped white onion
  • 2 medium jalapeño peppers
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1 tsp. salt

Directions

  1. Combine the chopped onion, jalapeño peppers, cilantro, lime juice and salt together in a medium mixing bowl. Set that aside to marinate while you seed and dice the tomatoes.
  2. Once all the tomatoes have been diced, place in the bowl with the rest of the ingredients and stir delicately until well combined.
  3. Place your pico de gallo in the fridge to marinate for at least 15 minutes or up to several hours to allow flavors to fully develop.
  4. Transfer to serving bowl with the help of a slotted spoon to leave excess liquid behind.
  5. Pico de gallo is best eaten fresh but will keep well in the refrigerator for up to a few days.
